This Website is designed to display the Nutrition Information of all Iems across Famous Franchises in NewYork

ERD diagram represents the entity relationships between different classes

  • Restaurants and Menu have one to many relationship with each other
  • An Item in a Menu has zero or multiple Nutrition information and Nutrition information has one to one relationship with Menu Items
  • Restaurants and Food category have one to many relationship with each other
